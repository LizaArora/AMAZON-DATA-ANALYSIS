# 📊 Amazon Sales Data Analysis

A data analytics project that transforms raw Amazon global sales data (2012–2015) into actionable business strategy — analyzing regional performance, product segments, and profitability to drive strategic recommendations.

## 📊 AI PM Case Study

This project was built applying AI Product Management thinking — treating data as a product and translating raw numbers into actionable business decisions through PM reasoning (RICE prioritization, stakeholder analysis, ethics, trade-offs).

**📄 [Read the Full Case Study (PDF)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Vy7nVKnU8uUhS_fNyYKykuiorxPFg6nD/view)**

**Key PM Decisions:**
- Flagged a critical 2013 data anomaly before drawing any conclusions — data integrity first
- Used RICE scoring to prioritize 5 strategic recommendations
- Applied BCG Matrix thinking to product portfolio (Stars, Cash Cows, Question Marks, Dogs)
- Recommended regional investment strategy based on growth vs. market share trade-offs

---

## 📌 What This Project Covers

### 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis
- Sales distribution across North America, Asia, Europe, LATAM, and Africa
- Growth trend identification by region
- Investment prioritization recommendations

### 📈 Segment & Product Analysis
- Sales breakdown by product segment
- Profit analysis by product line
- Portfolio optimization strategy (invest / maintain / phase out)

### 🔍 Data Quality & Anomaly Detection
- Time-series analysis (2012–2015)
- Identification of data inconsistencies
- Hypotheses for root-cause investigation

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Profit analysis by customer
- Customer segmentation framework (Platinum/Gold/Silver/Bronze)
- Retention strategy recommendations
